The Story

Charles Lillo started Assisting Seniors in June 2008 — not as a franchise acquisition or a business pivot, but because he saw families in Pensacola struggling to find non-medical home care they could actually trust. Seventeen years later, that hasn't changed. Neither has his approach.

He personally oversees the matching of every caregiver to every client. Not an algorithm, not an intake coordinator — him. Because he knows that the difference between a caregiver who works and one who becomes part of the family is compatibility, and compatibility isn't something you can process through a call center.

Assisting Seniors serves clients in their homes, in assisted living facilities, in nursing homes, and in memory care units — from 3-hour shifts to 24/7 coverage. They accept most long-term care insurance policies. And their caregivers keep coming back. Some have been with the same clients for years.

Who We Are

CareCircle Network is a senior care referral service owned and operated by TransBid LLC (DBA CareCircle Network), based in Pensacola, Florida. CareCircle Network is an independent platform and is not owned by or affiliated with any care agency listed in our network.

Information We Collect

When you submit the care-finder form or the agency application form, we collect the information you provide — including name, phone number, email address, city/zip, and care preferences. We do not require account creation.

How We Use Your Information

  • To match you with licensed senior care providers in your area
  • To contact you about your inquiry via phone or email
  • To share your contact information with matched partner agencies (families only)
  • To evaluate agency partner applications

How We Do NOT Use Your Information

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties
  • We do not share your information with agencies outside your service area
  • We do not use your information for advertising or retargeting

Compensation Disclosure

CareCircle Network is compensated by care provider partners for qualified referrals. Families are never charged. This compensation may influence which providers appear as "Featured" in our network.
