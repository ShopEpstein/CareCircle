Role Type 04 — Premium
Healthcare Relationship Closer
This is the highest-paid role in the organization. Designed for one type of person: someone who spent 10–25 years in healthcare, home care, senior living, hospice, or pharmaceutical sales and left behind a Rolodex of decision-makers who trust them personally. They aren't cold calling. They're calling people who will pick up on the first ring. We pay a significant premium because the cost of acquisition with a connector is near zero and the ticket size is high. If you know 20 ALF administrators in the Panhandle personally — this role is built for you.

Roles 06 & 07 — Leadership
Override & Management Structures
For contractors who build teams, recruit, or manage other reps. Overrides are paid on top of — never instead of — personal commission. A team lead who closes their own deals still earns full personal commission plus an override percentage on every rep they manage. All leadership roles are earned, not appointed. You get the title when you hit the threshold.
Team Lead · Override · Leadership
Sales Lead (Team Captain)
Manages 2–5 active reps in their team. Responsible for onboarding new reps, weekly activity reviews, and hitting team revenue targets. Earns override on every rep's commission payout. Personal selling required — you don't stop closing to manage.
