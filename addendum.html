1. Purpose

This Addendum modifies and supplements the Independent Contractor Agreement ("Agreement") previously entered into between TransBid LLC (DBA CareCircle Network) and the Contractor named above. All terms of the original Agreement remain in full force and effect except as expressly amended herein. In the event of any conflict between this Addendum and the original Agreement, this Addendum controls.

2. Role Designation

Contractor is designated as a Senior Relationship Closer — Business Consulting Services. This designation reflects Contractor's existing professional relationships and ability to source and close new agency partnerships on behalf of CareCircle Network.

3. Commission Structure

Notwithstanding the standard commission schedule set forth in the original Agreement, Contractor's commission rates for all business consulting service placements are as follows:

Commission TypeRateApplies To
Setup / Placement Fee60%One-time, upon partner activation
Monthly Recurring45%Each month the account remains active and paid

All other commission terms (payment timing, clawback on setup fees within 60 days, tier definitions) are as stated in the original Agreement unless modified elsewhere in this Addendum.

4. Perpetual Recurring Commission

Special Term

Contractor's 45% monthly recurring commissions on accounts sourced under this Addendum shall continue for as long as those accounts remain active and paying, regardless of whether Contractor's agreement with CareCircle Network remains active. Section 11.4 of the original Agreement (which provides that recurring commissions cease at termination) does not apply to accounts closed under this Addendum.

This perpetual recurring right is personal to Contractor and is not transferable. CareCircle Network's obligation to pay recurring commissions is contingent solely on the continued activity and payment of the referred account — not on any ongoing work obligation by Contractor.

5. Weekly Activity Report Exemption

Special Term

Contractor is exempt from the weekly activity report requirement set forth in the original Agreement. Section 6.2 of the original Agreement (which provides that recurring commissions may be held for late reports) does not apply to Contractor. All commissions earned are paid on the standard schedule without condition on report submission.

6. Entire Agreement

This Addendum, together with the original Independent Contractor Agreement, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to its subject matter and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, or agreements. This Addendum may not be modified except in writing signed by both parties.

Effective Date: This Addendum is effective as of the date it is signed by Contractor below. Company signature is confirmed by issuance of this document.
